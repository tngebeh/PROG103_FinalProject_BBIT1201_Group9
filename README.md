# Smart Business Management System (SBMS)
The **Smart Business Management System (SBMS)** is a desktop-based business automation solution designed to simplify and improve the management of essential business activities for Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Sierra Leone. 
Built using Python, Tkinter, and SQLite, the platform centralizes operations to eliminate human error, tracking delays, and fragmented record-keeping associated with traditional manual ledgers.


##Key Features

* **Inventory & Product Management:** Automatic stock-level tracking with predefined low-stock threshold alerts to prevent shortages.
* **Customer & Vendor Modules:** Centralized database profiles to store contact logs and optimize procurement streams.
* **Order Processing:** Automated transaction routing, price calculations, and immediate inventory adjustments upon purchase.
* **Payment Tracking:** Records financial transactions supporting multiple payment methods (Cash, Mobile Money, Bank Transfer).
* **Expense Recording & Analytics:** Direct logging of operational outlays mapped to automated profit/loss reporting tools.
* **Automated Reporting:** Generates instant sales, expense, and profit reports to provide leadership with data-driven insights.


##Built With

The architecture follows strict **Structured Programming Principles** (modular design, clear data scopes, and sequential logic execution):
* **Language:** Python 3
* **GUI Framework:** Tkinter
* **Database Engine:** SQLite (`business.db`)


##Privacy & Legal Compliance

Engineered with careful adherence to regional regulatory frameworks governing information security and digital privacy in Sierra Leone:
* **Data Minimization:** Built in alignment with the *Sierra Leone Data Protection and Right to Access Information Policy Framework*, processing only inputs functionally necessary for business computing.
* **Constitutional Privacy Rights:** Respects Section 22 of the *Constitution of Sierra Leone 1991* by ensuring transient session variables remain securely in volatile memory (RAM) and clear safely upon application exit.
* **Database Relational Scoping:** System access is strictly brokered by a dedicated backend layer to prevent cross-contamination, with zero hardcoded credentials inside public open-source repository paths.
